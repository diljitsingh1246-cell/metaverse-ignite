About Metaverse Ignite

“Metaverse Ignite” is the annual college fest that celebrates creativity, innovation, and talent. Hosted by our vibrant student community, the fest bridges the gap between the digital and physical worlds — offering a blend of art, technology, and culture.

From graphic design challenges to soulful performances, we invite students from all disciplines to showcase their skills, imagination, and teamwork spirit.
